COVID-19 Korea Dashboard

Comprehensive Visualizations of COVID-19 Data in South Korea

Map of Cases by Province and Exact Location

This map provides a geographical representation of COVID-19 cases across South Korea, pinpointing exact locations with latitude and longitude coordinates.
Use the controls and legend to filter and explore the data interactively.

Sunburst Chart of Cases by Age Group, Gender, and Infection Case

Explore the breakdown of COVID-19 cases in South Korea by age groups, gender, and specific infection cases using this interactive sunburst chart.
Click on segments to drill down into more detailed layers of the data.

Hierarchical Tree Map of Cases by Cities and Provinces

This treemap visualizes the distribution of confirmed COVID-19 cases across various cities and provinces in South Korea.
Hover over the rectangles to see detailed information, and use the breadcrumb navigation to explore different levels of the hierarchy.

Network of COVID-19 Cases

This force-directed graph shows the relationships between patients and infection cases in South Korea.
Use the filter to explore specific infection cases and see their connections. Nodes can be dragged to rearrange the network layout.

COVID-19 Cases Timeline

Interactive timeline showing the progression of confirmed, released, and deceased COVID-19 cases over time in South Korea.
Use the play button to animate the visualization or manually control the progress with the slider.
Hover over lines for detailed information.
Note: The timeline data is shown on a logarithmic scale to better visualize the trends, instead of the actual case numbers.
